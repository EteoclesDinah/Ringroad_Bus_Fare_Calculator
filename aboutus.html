About Our App

The ring road bus fare calculator app is a mobile application designed to assist users in calculating and determining the fares for bus trips in ring road of Kathmandu Valley consisting of the major routes and stops from Balkhu, Sanepa, Ekantakuna, Satdobato, Gwarko, Koteshor, Sinamangal, Gaushala, Chabahil, Chakrapath, Balaju, Banasthali, Swoyambhu, Kalanki and back to Balkhu.These are the major stops and there are other many stops and station within them which will be intregated into the system later on.

It provides a user-friendly interface where users can input their travel details,such as the starting and ending locations, to obtain the total distance and accurate fare information based on the selected route.This app retrieves fare information specific to the chosen route,taking into account factors like distance,fare types, and any applicable discounts.Users can also create personal profiles and save their favorite routes for quick access in the future.



How To Use App?

Follow The Following Steps!!

Steps:

1:Enter your Origin and Destination .
2:Tap on Fare Calculate Button.
3:Login into app,if you are new to app then signup first.
4:View the total fare.
5:Proceed payment by cash or Scan QR code .
